About CommsCards

Welcome to the Univesity of Dundee 'CommsCards' app.

This app is designed to aid students with their Communication Skills by giving a variety of Flash Cards to practice their skills against.

On the front of the card there is a brief clinical vignette, and on the back an 'ideal' answer.

Also avaliable are full size OSCE Scenarios: complete with a student question, patient script, and expectations.

These should ideally be used once a student is comfortable with their skills.

Lawrence Adams 2017
