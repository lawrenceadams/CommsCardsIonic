About CommsCards

Welcome to the Univesity of Dundee 'CommsCards' app.

This app is a teaching aid designed for students to practice their Communication Skills with the aid of Flash Cards as well as OSCE Scenarios.

On the front of the card there is a brief clinical vignette, and on the back an 'ideal' answer.

Also avaliable are full size OSCE Scenarios: complete with a student question, patient script, and what is expected from the student.

Within the 'Useful Links' section, there are both links to the relevant section of MedBlogs and Videos to aid Clinical Skills practice.
